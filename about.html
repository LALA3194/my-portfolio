About me

I have dedicated my whole life to being in a classroom. I have a bachelors in elementary education, with a minor in psychology and a master's in curriculum and instruction. During my off time, I obtained the TFLE and Green Educator certificates. The dream of being in the classroom was cut short this year, due to developing severe asthma.

By May of 2022, I was hospitalized and doctors advised a career change. By June 2022, I found the perfect environment for my lungs was behind a computer screen. I have equipped myself with front-end development tools through the SheCodes Bootcamp in just four months. In addition, I will be adding UX/UI design tools in hopes of being a well-rounded front-end developer.
